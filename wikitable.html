Welcome

Flipkart is an Indian e-commerce company headquartered in Bangalore, Karnataka. It was founded in 2007 by Sachin Bansal and Binny Bansal. Flipkart started as an online bookstore but quickly expanded its product offerings to include electronics, fashion, home appliances, and various other categories. Flipkart operates an online marketplace where sellers can list their products and customers can purchase them. It offers a wide range of products, including mobile phones, laptops, clothing, accessories, home decor, furniture, and more. The platform provides a user-friendly interface for customers to browse and buy products, with multiple payment options such as cash on delivery, credit/debit cards, and net banking. Flipkart has gained significant popularity in India and has played a crucial role in the growth of e-commerce in the country. It has also launched its own private label brands and introduced services like Flipkart Plus, which offers loyalty benefits to customers. Flipkart has invested in building a robust supply chain and logistics network to ensure efficient delivery of products across the country. In 2018, Walmart, the American multinational retail corporation, acquired a majority stake in Flipkart. This partnership has provided Flipkart with access to Walmart's global expertise and resources while enabling Walmart to expand its presence in the Indian market. Overall, Flipkart is one of the leading e-commerce platforms in India, offering a wide range of products and services to customers across the country.
